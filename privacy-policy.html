Section 01

Introduction & Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications ("Apps") developed and published by Khayyal K. ("we", "us", or "our"), available on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

By downloading or using any of our Apps, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

This is a general policy that covers all our apps. Some apps may have their own supplemental privacy policy for app-specific features.
Section 02

Information We Do Not Collect

Our Apps are designed with privacy in mind. We do not:

  • Require you to create an account or register
  • Collect your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Access your contacts, camera, microphone, or location (unless explicitly required for a specific app feature and disclosed at the time of permission request)
  • Track you across other apps or websites
  • Sell your data to any third party
Section 03

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Most of our Apps store preference and progress data locally on your device only, using the platform's standard storage (UserDefaults on iOS, SharedPreferences on Android). This typically includes:

  • App settings and preferences (e.g. language, sound on/off)
  • Game progress, scores, and unlocked content
  • In-app currency balances
This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to our servers and is not accessible to us. Deleting the App removes this data permanently.
Section 04

Third-Party Services

Our Apps may integrate third-party services that have their own privacy practices. These may include:

Google AdMob (Advertising)
Apps that display advertisements use Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve relevant ads. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

  • Google Privacy Policy →
  • How Google uses AdMob data →

Apple / Google Platform Services
Our Apps run on iOS and Android platforms. Apple and Google may collect diagnostic and performance data under their own privacy policies, independent of our Apps.

We carefully select third-party services and only include those necessary for core app functionality (advertising, analytics). We do not integrate social networks, tracking SDKs, or data brokers.
Section 05

Advertising & Opt-Out

Apps that are free to download are supported by advertising. We use Google AdMob to display ads. You can limit ad personalisation at any time:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalisation"

Opting out means you may see less relevant ads, but the App will continue to function normally. Ads help us keep our Apps free for everyone.

Section 06

Rewarded Advertisements

Some of our Apps offer optional rewarded video advertisements in exchange for in-app benefits (such as virtual currency or extra lives). These are always voluntary — you are never required to watch an ad to use the core features of the App.

If a rewarded ad fails to load, the in-app reward is still granted to avoid penalising you for network or ad availability issues.

Section 07

Encryption

All network communications made by our Apps (including ad requests and any optional analytics) use industry-standard HTTPS/TLS encryption provided by the iOS and Android operating systems. We do not implement custom or proprietary encryption algorithms.

Our Apps qualify for the standard encryption exemption under US Export Administration Regulations (EAR) as they use only standard OS-provided encryption for HTTPS communications.

Section 08

Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in some jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through one of our Apps, please contact us at khayyal@kherais.com and we will promptly delete any such information.

Section 09

Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is nothing to retain on our end. Any data stored locally on your device is retained until you uninstall the App, at which point it is permanently deleted by the operating system.

Data collected by third-party services (such as Google AdMob) is subject to their own retention policies.

Section 10

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including access, correction, deletion, and portability of your personal data. As we do not collect personal data ourselves, these rights apply primarily to data held by our third-party service providers.

  • Google data: myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy →
  • Apple data: privacy.apple.com →
  • Contact us: khayyal@kherais.com

For residents of the EU/EEA (GDPR), California (CCPA), or Saudi Arabia (PDPL), you may submit requests to exercise your rights by emailing us directly.

Section 11

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our Apps, legal requirements, or third-party services. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of any of our Apps after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Section 12

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the privacy practices of any of our Apps, please contact us:
