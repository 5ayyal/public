Effective Date: April 24, 2026

Section 01

Introduction

Welcome to Falconer: Desert Sky Runner ("the App"), developed and operated by Khayyal Kherais ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

By downloading or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Section 02

Information We Collect

The App itself does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require account creation, login, or any form of registration.

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Your best score and current score
  • Feather currency balance
  • Equipped and unlocked falcon skins
  • Language preference (English or Arabic)
  • Audio mute preference
This data never leaves your device. It is not sent to any server and is not accessible to us.
Section 03

Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service operated by Google LLC. AdMob may collect and use certain information to serve personalised or contextual advertisements, including:

  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS / GAID on Android)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device type, operating system, and language
  • App usage data and ad interaction data

This data collection is governed by Google's own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them:

Google Privacy Policy →
How Google uses data in AdMob →

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time through your device settings:
iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
Section 04

Rewarded Advertisements

The App offers optional rewarded video advertisements. Watching these ads is entirely voluntary — they are offered in exchange for in-game rewards (feathers currency or falcon revival). You are never required to watch an ad to continue playing.

Rewarded ads are also served by Google AdMob and are subject to the same data practices described in Section 03.

Section 05

Encryption

The App uses standard HTTPS/TLS encryption for all network communications, including ad requests. This is the industry-standard encryption provided by the iOS operating system. We do not implement any proprietary or custom encryption algorithms.

Section 06

Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Note that Google AdMob may serve ads based on contextual signals. For family-directed apps, AdMob can be configured to serve non-personalised ads only.

Section 07

Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is no data retention on our end. All game data stored locally on your device can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the App.

Section 08

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data. Since we do not collect personal data ourselves, these rights primarily apply to data held by Google AdMob. You may exercise those rights directly through Google's privacy controls at:

myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy →
